Getting started in the FinOps Foundation

There are many ways to meet community members and get involved. Here are some recommendations based on our most successful and collaborative members.

First, be sure to join as a member. Next, review the suggestions listed below, sorted by FinOps experience:

I’m new to FinOps and I have questions and want to get started

  • Join our Slack and ask a first question in the #ask-a-question channel
  • Learn from the stories and real-world FinOps experiences from our members via our Community User Stories
  • Check out our Adopting FinOps Project to get inspiration on how to start building your practice
  • Watch our recent Vendor Showcase to learn about solutions if you're looking for FinOps tooling
  • Get the official book on Cloud FinOps
  • Sign up for training and get your FinOps Certified Practitioner Badge
  • Still need help finding what you need? Let us know at hello@finops.org.

I’m here to take my FinOps skills to the next level

  • Engage with your peers on Slack (there are numerous channels by topic)
  • Learn the latest FinOps trends and insights from the latest State of FinOps Survey data
  • Join the closest FinOps event or meetup near you
  • Get practical guidance and knowledge from our collection of FinOps Projects and Playbooks
  • Take your skills to the next level with the FinOps Certified Professional course
  • Catch up on previous Summits and other resources
  • Assess your company’s FinOps maturity against the FinOps Maturity Model
  • Learn about key FinOps Capabilities that you'd should improve at your organization
  • Stay up to date on FinOps jobs on #Jobs Slack Channel

I want to contribute to the FinOps Foundation and help others

  • Engage with peers and answer their questions in our community Slack
  • Join a Working Group and work on interesting Projects and Resources
  • Contribute edits and updates to the FinOps Framework
  • Attend a Technical Advisory Council meeting
  • Learn about the FinOps Ambassador Program and apply for membership today

If you are looking for something specific, please ask the community in #ask-a-question on Slack or email the staff at hello@finops.org.
