Getting started in the FinOps Foundation

Want suggestions on how to engage with the FinOps Foundation Community? First, be sure to join as a member. Next, review the suggestions listed below sorted by your potential stage/interest:

I’m new to FinOps and I have questions

  • Join our Slack and ask your question on #ask-a-question
  • Learn from others in Real World Member Stories
  • Find help Adopting FinOps
  • Gain insights on Getting Engineers to Take Action
  • Learn about vendors in our community
  • Get the official book on Cloud FinOps
  • Sign up for training and get your FinOps Certified Practitioner Badge
  • Still need help finding what you need? Let us know at hello@finops.org.

I’m here to take my FinOps skills to the next level

  • Engage with your peers on Slack
  • Join the closest Meetup near you
  • Take your skills to the next level with the FinOps Certified Professional course
  • Catch up on previous summits and other resources
  • I want to assess my company’s FinOps maturity: FinOps Maturity Model
  • Learn about the Capabilities that I need to progress in
  • Stay up to date on FinOps jobs on #Jobs Slack Channel

I want to contribute

  • Engage with peers and answer questions in the community Slack
  • Join a Working Group
  • Contribute and update to the Framework
  • Add to the Reducing Waste library
  • Attend a Technical Advisory Council meeting
  • Learn about the Ambassador Program (community feedback welcome, coming soon), or apply today

If you are looking for something specific you can ask the community in #ask-a-question on Slack or email the staff at hello@finops.org.
