product-overview: |
  Leverage automation to assure your applications always consume exactly what they need to perform, reducing cloud and IT spend, as well as your carbon footprint.

  Want to get your cloud spend under control without compromising your customers’ digital experience? The only solution is to have software that continuously resources applications so that they get exactly what they need when they need it. Turbonomic works through APIs to discover your environment, mapping out the resource relationships across applications and infrastructure, in the cloud and on-prem. With this full-stack understanding, Turbonomic continuously generates resource actions that customers safely automate, allowing IT teams to focus on innovation and FinOps teams to ensure continuous business value from the cloud.

  Automation you can operationalize. Automation at scale necessitates a proactive approach. Turbonomic automation can integrate with any pipeline, Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC), ITSM, or communication tool in your organization. Operationalizing automation allows IT teams to deliver measurable results immediately and continuously.

  Turbonomic can optimize your cloud and IT estate wherever your applications run. Turbonomic is technology agnostic, allowing customers to use our automation whether they are leveraging hybrid cloud, multicloud, or containerized environments. For customers looking to leverage cloud native and PaaS services for modern applications, Turbonomic can elastically scale based on your organization’s service level objectives (SLOs).
