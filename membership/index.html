Eligibility

To be considered for charter or practitioner community membership you are required to work in one of the primary roles in FinOps: 1) Executive/Senior IT roles, 2) Executive/Senior Finance roles, 3) Cloud financial or business analysts or 4) Cloud engineering or architecture-related role, etc. We recognize that many titles do FinOps so if you’re unsure please submit a short note with your application explaining how your job relates to FinOps. Those in a sales, marketing, or business development role aren’t eligible for membership.
